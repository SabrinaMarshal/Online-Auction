FAQs

Why use our site for online auctioning?

If you are a bidder, and looking for a genuine online bidding site, this site is the best place. Our site has a variety of items, and is split into categories for easo of shopping.

Is it a dynamic auction or a blind auction ?

It is a dynamic auction, a bidder can see the highest bid at any point of time.

How to bid on items?

On the navigation bar, choose bid on items. Choose the appropriate category and click on the product you want to bid on. The current maximum bid will be shown. Enter a higher bid and wait for auction results.

Does each auction last for the same time?

Nope, the time for auction depends on the preference of the person who put up the item to auction.
